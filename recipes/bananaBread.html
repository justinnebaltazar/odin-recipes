Banana Bread

Why this Banana Bread Recipe?

This banana bread recipe greates the most delicious, moist load with loads of banana flavour. Why compromise the banana flavour!? Friends and family love this recipe and say it is by far the best. This banana bread recipe will become your go-to recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 deg. C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  4. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, approx. 60 minutes.
  6. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
